My name is Benny Burwell. I paint with oils and watercolor. For as long as I can remember I have been working toward improving my art. You can never stop learning new things in the art world and that is why it intrigues me so much. I always am trying to push myself with new techniques, styles, and materials.

Learning from myself, friends, and favorite artsits from around the world is how I have developed my style. I have completed my Associates in Visual Fine Art
